## Video Recommendations

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended for best browser compatibility)
- **Orientation**: Vertical (9:16 ratio) or Square (1:1) for mobile-first design
- **Duration**: 5-15 seconds (short, engaging clips work best)
- **Resolution**: 720p or 1080p
- **File size**: Keep under 5MB per video for fast loading on mobile data

## Tips

- Show the dish being prepared, plated, or in an appetizing close-up
- Use good lighting to make the food look appealing
- Keep videos short and loopable
- Consider adding subtle background music (ensure you have rights to use it)
